Ghast Sounds

Ghast sounds are made by C418's Cats!

C418 is the person who made the sounds for Minecraft.


Lava flowing

In the Overworld, lava flows at 50% of the speed of water.

But in the Nether, it flows at 80%, so watch out!


Paused Overworld

In single-player Minecraft in the Nether, the Overworld gets paused.

So crops don't grow, mobs don't move, and time doesn't pass, alongside other things.


Rare biomes

The Mesa is the rarest biome in the Overworld.

The Mushroom Island is the second rarest.

Death Messages

When you die to an explosion, the death message "MCHubSite was blown up by ______" will appear in chat.

When you die to a melee attack, the death message "MCHubSite was slain by ______" will appear in chat.

When you die to a projectile, the death message "MCHubSite was shot by ______" will appear in chat.

When you burn to death, the death message "MCHubSite burnt to a crisp" will appear in the chat.

When you die to magma, the death message "MCHubSite discovered the floor was lava" will appear in the chat.

When you die to an exploding bed, the death message "MCHubSite was slain by [Intentional Game Design]" will appear in the chat.

When you fly into a wall and die, the death message "MCHubSite experienced kinetic energy" will appear in the chat.

When you fall to your death, the death message "MCHubSite fell from a high place" will appear in the chat.

When you drown, the death message "MCHubSite drowned" will appear in the chat.

When you die to potions, the death message "MCHubSite was killed by magic" will appear in the chat.

When you fall into the void, the death message "MCHubSite fell out of the world" will appear in the chat.

For Bedrock edition only.
