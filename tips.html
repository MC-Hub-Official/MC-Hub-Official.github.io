The fastest ways to move

In Survival, the fastest way to move is sprinting while jumping roughly every second, standing on ice with trapdoors above you. The effects of Speed help too.

When on the move, avoid soul, slime and honey blocks. Crouching will slow you down too, as well as the effects of Slowness.

In Creative mode, try sprinting while flying. You go really fast. Soul Speed on boots will speed you up on soul blocks.


Find ore

One of the best ways to find ore in Minecraft is to start a strip mine.

A strip mine is a long horizontal mine at a specific Y level.

For diamonds, it's best to start from Y levels 1-15. For iron, try Y level 50.

For coal, level 60. AAAANND for Netherite (Ancient Debris) try levels 15-17 in the Nether.

You can also find diamonds in village chests and other chests.


Never mine straight down

Never mine straight down in Survival. There are many lava lakes and mob-filled caves you could fall into.

If you absolutely have to, stand in the middle of two blocks, mine one, then the other, in alternate patterns.

This will allow you to see what's below you before you fall in.


The best ways to fight a blaze

Build a complete wall around the blaze spawner allowing them no space. Don't forget the roof.

Then go outside the wall and break one block, and only one. Then you can attack them through the hole.


Bring water buckets to the End

You can use them to surround yourself in water when endermen are attacking you.

Endermen are allergic to water so the will then not be able to get to you.

You can also use them to break your fall off an obsidian pillar.

You can also use Feather Falling on boots to break your fall, but you will still take some damage.


Bookshelves enchance your enchantment table

You can put 15 bookshelves in a 1-block high wall around your table to make it look especially nice.

This will greatly enchance the power of the table and give you much better enchantments.

Leave one block for the entrance. There are many other bookshelf formations that will look equally - or more - nice.


Always wear golden armour to the Nether

Always wear at least one piece of golden armour when entering the Nether.

Piglins do not attack you when you wear golden armour unless you attack them or mine nearby Nether Gold.
